Common Questions

What do I actually do?

Sign up, share which school your athlete attends, and answer whether you support sanctioning. That's your contribution to the movement. From there, you can generate materials, see progress updates, and connect with other families.

What do clubs do?

Club leaders share their numbers once per season‚Äîtotal athletes, gender breakdown, schools represented. Simple counts that help complete the statewide picture.

Is this another club management app?

No. We don't do registration, schedules, or team chat. Kinetic Team brings families and clubs together around one shared goal‚Äîgetting your sport sanctioned. Your club tools and Kinetic Team work side by side.

Why would clubs participate?

Because families will invite them. And because clubs want sanctioning too‚Äîit benefits everyone. Plus, clubs get visibility into statewide progress and materials they can use for their own advocacy.

Is it free?

Yes, during this founding period. We're building this together, and founding families will always be recognized for being part of the beginning.

Why Nebraska lacrosse first?

You're an organized, passionate community already working toward NSAA sanctioning. You're the perfect partners to build this with‚Äîand you need this now.

When can I start?

We're building now and inviting founding families to join. Sign up below to be part of the first wave‚Äîyour feedback will shape everything we create.
