Common Questions

What does the athlete actually do?

A 2-minute check-in. Note how you slept, your energy, what's on your mind. That's it.

What do parents see?

Weekly summaries showing topic frequency and themes‚Äîhow often certain words came up. Not the actual check-in text (unless the athlete shares it).

What do coaches see?

Nothing. This is a family tool only.

Does Kinetic Team tell me what's wrong or what to do?

No. We show you patterns from your own check-ins. We don't interpret them, diagnose anything, or suggest actions. You decide what the information means.

What makes Kinetic Team different from other apps?

Sports-specific language processing (we understand athlete talk), no scores ever, personal baseline comparison (not population averages), privacy-first design (parents see trends, not entries), and no competition or leaderboards.

What data can I note in my check-ins?

Sleep, energy, mood, open notes, context tags (school/practice/games), physical notes, and optional HealthKit integration for device data. 7 dimensions in 2 minutes.
