Common Questions

What do parents actually do?

Sign up, enter which school your athlete attends, answer whether you support sanctioning. That's the core. Then you can generate one-pagers for your AD, see advocacy updates, and share news with other families.

What do clubs do?

Club reps enter aggregate numbers once per season: total athletes, gender breakdown, and which schools are represented. No roster management, no names‚Äîjust counts for advocacy.

Does this replace TeamSnap or SportsEngine?

No. Those platforms run your club operations (schedules, payments, communication). We do the one thing they don't: aggregate data across all clubs and turn it into a sanctioning campaign.

Why would clubs join?

Because parents will ask them to. And because the platform generates reports that help clubs too‚Äîboard presentations, advocacy materials, visibility into statewide growth.

Is it free?

Yes, during beta. We're still figuring out the right model. Beta families will have input on pricing and get founding member benefits.
