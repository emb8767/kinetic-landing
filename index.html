Common Questions

What do athletes actually do?

A quick 2-minute check-in each day. Rate sleep, energy, stress, and mood‚Äîplus an optional note. It's like a private journal that builds self-awareness over time.

Can my teen see what I see?

They know you see trends, not their raw entries. We've designed it so teens feel safe being honest, and parents get meaningful insights without surveillance.

What if my teen doesn't want to do it?

We've designed check-ins to feel low-pressure‚Äîmore like a personal journal than homework. Most teens actually appreciate having a way to express themselves without face-to-face conversation.

Does the coach see anything?

Absolutely not. This is 100% for families. Coaches never have access to any wellness data. We're not a performance tracking tool‚Äîwe're a family wellness tool.
